Get Involved in Leadership

We welcome community members who feel called to serve in various ministry areas. Leadership opportunities include:

  • Worship leadership and liturgy
  • Community outreach and social justice
  • Family and children's ministries

  • Session (Board of Elders) service
  • Ministry team coordination

Contact our ministry leaders to learn about current opportunities to serve and lead in our community.
