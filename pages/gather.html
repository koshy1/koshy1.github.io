What to Expect

Our worship services are welcoming and inclusive, designed for people at all stages of their spiritual journey. Whether you're new to church or have been attending for years, you'll find a place at our table.

  • Meaningful worship with contemporary and traditional elements
  • Community prayers and shared concerns
  • Inclusive language and practices
  • Child-friendly environment
  • Accessible facilities and online options

First Time Visiting?

We'd love to welcome you! No need to dress up or know anything special - just come as you are. Our community is excited to meet you and share in worship together.
