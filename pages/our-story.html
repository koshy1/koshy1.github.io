Our Story

The journey of Bethel Community Presbyterian Church

Our Foundation

Bethel Community Presbyterian Church formed from a merger of two churches in 1955. Originally, we had over 300 members with thriving community ministries that served our San Leandro neighborhood.

Growth and Service

In 1998, Rev. Sarah Reyes Gibbs led redevelopment efforts that transformed our community engagement. Under her leadership, we:

  • Started the San Leandro Community Food Pantry
  • Formed meaningful interfaith partnerships
  • Collaborated with Spanish-speaking ministry

Discernment and Partnership

Between 2017-2018, our church underwent strategic discernment about its future direction. This led us to partner with First Presbyterian Church of Hayward for revitalization efforts.

In May 2019, the San Francisco Presbytery approved this partnership, marking a new chapter in our community's journey.

Current Leadership

Michael and Erina Kim-Eubanks became our co-ministry leaders, bringing fresh vision and energy to our community.

Our Future

"While the future of Bethel Community is unknown, it is clear that the Holy Spirit has never stopped working in and through the Bethel Community!"
