Our Partners

Building community through collaboration and shared ministry

Church Partnerships

First Presbyterian Church of Hayward

In May 2019, the San Francisco Presbytery approved our partnership with First Presbyterian Church of Hayward for revitalization efforts. This collaboration has brought new energy and resources to our ministry.

Community Partnerships

San Leandro Community Food Pantry

Founded by Rev. Sarah Reyes Gibbs in 1998, the San Leandro Community Food Pantry continues to serve our neighbors in need. This vital ministry addresses food insecurity in our local community.

Interfaith Partnerships

We actively collaborate with other faith communities in San Leandro and the broader East Bay area to work together on social justice initiatives and community service.

Spanish-Speaking Ministry Collaboration

We partner with Spanish-speaking congregations to create inclusive ministry opportunities that bridge language and cultural differences.

Denominational Connections

San Francisco Presbytery

As part of the San Francisco Presbytery, we participate in regional Presbyterian Church (USA) initiatives and benefit from shared resources and support.

Presbyterian Church (USA)

We are proud members of the PC(USA), sharing in the denomination's commitment to progressive Christianity, social justice, and inclusive ministry.

Get Involved

Interested in partnering with Bethel Community or learning more about our collaborative ministries? We welcome new partnerships that align with our values of justice, inclusion, and community service.

Contact us at info@bethelcommunitySL.org to explore partnership opportunities.
