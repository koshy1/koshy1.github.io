Our Philosophy of Generosity

"As a faith community, we practice generosity as a way of acknowledging that God has been generous to each one of us."

We encourage members to freely give their time, skills, money, and belongings in response to God, and for the sake of our communities. Your generous support helps Bethel Community continue our ministry of welcome, justice, and spiritual growth.
