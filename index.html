Beth-el: House of God.

We believe that church is meant to be more than an institution, a building, or belief system.

We are a community reimagining church around God's great welcome table.

Bethel Community is a justice-oriented, open and affirming PC(USA) church and we welcome the full inclusion of all people in the life and ministries of our church as we journey toward reconciliation through Christ.
