Giving at Bethel Community

As a faith community, we practice generosity as a way of acknowledging that God has been generous to each one of us. We encourage our community members to freely give their time, skills, money, and belongings in response to God, and for the sake of our communities.

There are several options available for those who wish to make a financial gift to Bethel Community:

ONLINE

You can make a one-time donation or setup a recurring donation on our secure online giving platform.

Make A Donation Online

MAIL

Checks can be written out to 'Bethel Community Presbyterian Church.' You can mail your financial gift to our church office:

14235 Bancroft Ave.
San Leandro, CA 94578

IN-PERSON

You can give your financial gift during our communal time of generosity at our Sunday Gathering.

Receive our Newsletter

Stay tuned to the latest Bethel Community news by signing up to receive our weekly email newsletter.
