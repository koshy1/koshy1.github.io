Our Gatherings

Sunday Worship Gatherings

We gather in person twice a month - usually on 2nd and 4th Sundays - for hybrid worship gatherings, both in person and on Zoom. Every other week we host All-Zoom worship gatherings. All services are at 10am (PST).

To see our health and safety guidelines for in-person worship gatherings, please click here.

Monthly Regional Groups

Connection groups meet in person either once or twice a month, as a space for smaller group community-building and connection through shared affinities or geographies. They run from September through May and take a break for the summer.

Click here for more info and group listings.

2025 Fall Formation Groups

Formation groups meet weekly on Zoom for a set period of time and provide opportunities for spiritual and theological formation in community. Members are asked to attend as consistently as possible and stay through the duration of the group.

Fall formation groups will run between 8-10 weeks and begin in mid-September and run until the week before Thanksgiving. Sign up here!

Available Groups:

  • Discerning the Way with Barney + Jazzy, Wednesdays @ 7pm, 9/17-11/19
  • Discerning the Way with Pastor Michael + Yehsong, Mondays @ 8pm, 9/11-11/17
  • Rediscovering Prayer with Pastor Erina, Tuesdays @ 8pm, 9/12-11/18

Family Meet Ups

Every other month, we host a family meet up for both parents and kids at the church, a time for connection for the adults, and play for the kids. These events are usually on a Saturday morning from 10:30am-12pm.

Please contact Jill for latest days and times for the gathering.
